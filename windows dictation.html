What Is Windows Dictation?

Windows 10 and 11 come with a built-in speech-to-text feature. Just press Windows Key + H and start talking. Your words appear in any text field - Word documents, web browsers, chat windows, or LLM interfaces like ChatGPT.

Using with LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)

This is perfect for LLMs! AI models are designed to understand natural, conversational language - exactly what you get when speaking naturally. Instead of thinking about how to "type" a prompt, just talk to the AI like you're having a conversation.

Simple Workflow

  • Open your AI chat interface
  • Press Win + H to start dictation
  • Speak your prompt: "Explain quantum physics in simple terms"
  • Press Win + H again to stop
  • Hit Enter to send
  • Continue the conversation by voice!

Pro Tip: You can say "period," "comma," "question mark," and "new line" to add punctuation and formatting!

Voice Commands for Punctuation

  • Say "period" for a full stop
  • Say "comma" for a pause
  • Say "question mark" for questions
  • Say "new line" to break lines
  • Say "exclamation point" for emphasis
