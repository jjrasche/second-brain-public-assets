Common Questions

What about dietary restrictions and preferences? +
Our matching system groups families with compatible dietary needs. Whether you're vegan, gluten-free, or managing diabetes, you'll be paired with others who understand. Our AI meal planning ensures everyone's restrictions are respected in every meal.
What if I don't like cooking or I'm not good at it? +
Perfect! You only cook once every 4 days, and we provide simple, tested recipes. Many members say focusing on one meal actually improved their cooking. Plus, you can start with basics like pasta bakes or slow-cooker meals. The community shares tips and techniques.
How does meal delivery work? +
Meals are delivered in reusable containers between 5-6 PM. You'll drop off to your 3 pod neighbors (usually within a 5-minute walk). Insulated bags keep food at safe temperatures. After eating, rinse containers and leave in your collection bin for weekly pickup.
Can I skip weeks or take vacations? +
Absolutely! Life happens. Give your pod 1 week notice and we'll arrange coverage from our substitute pool. You can pause membership for up to 4 weeks per year for vacations. The flexibility is built into the system.
